History

Welcome to the history of our white water rafting company. our company was founded in 2005 by a group of experienced white water rafting enthusiasts who shared a passion for adventure and a love of the great outdoors. The founders of the company had all worked for other white water rafting outfits in the past, but they felt that they could offer something unique to the industry. They wanted to create a company that would provide an unforgettable rafting experience for their customers while maintaining a strong commitment to safety and the environment. In the early days, our company offered rafting trips on a single river in the Pacific Northwest. However, word quickly spread about the exciting and safe trips offered by our company, and demand for our services grew rapidly. Over the years, our company has expanded its operations to include trips on several rivers across the country. We have also added new services, such as overnight camping trips and team-building exercises for corporate groups. Despite this growth, our company has remained committed to its founding principles. Our guides are all highly experienced and trained in white water rescue techniques, ensuring that our customers are safe at all times. We also have a strong commitment to the environment, and we work closely with local conservation groups to ensure that the rivers we raft on remain pristine for future generations to enjoy. As we look to the future, our company plans to continue to expand its operations while maintaining its commitment to safety and the environment. We are excited to share the thrill of white water rafting with even more people and to help them create memories that will last a lifetime.
